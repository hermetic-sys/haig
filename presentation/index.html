The Framework

Separation of powers. Not separation of prompts.

Authority
The Human
Ratifies every decision. Sets constraints. Breaks deadlocks.
Cannot be overridden. By anything. Ever.
Architect
AI — Design Context
Drafts blueprints, defines gate checks. Pushes back when the Authority's request has a flaw.
CANNOT execute code. CANNOT approve own designs.
Auditor
AI — Adversarial (Different Model)
Attacks the Architect's designs. Finds what was missed.
CANNOT approve own findings. Fresh context only.
Executor
AI — Implementation
Writes code chunk by chunk against the blueprint. Runs gates. Reports results.
CANNOT make design decisions. CANNOT skip gate checks.

Each role operates in its own context. Cross-contamination isn't discouraged. It's impossible.
